Project Summary: The service/restaurant industry and the salaries, occupations, and diversity

Whether financial, political, or social -- The service and restaurant industry is a corner stone of our economy. Whether you are a person looking for a job, a restaurantuer looking for an ideal place to setup a new place, or a government employee looking at where public funds could be benificial for the industry.

Python, Flask, matplotlib, pandas, Javascript, and mongoDB were used in this project to visualize the restaurant industry to explore wages, occupations, and diversity in the United States.
